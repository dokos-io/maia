Discover Maia

The Midwife's activity management application

Install

From your site

To install this app, login to your site and click on "Installer". Search for Maia and click on "Install"

Using Bench

Go to your bench folder and setup the new app

$ bench get-app maia https://github.com/DOKOS-IO/maia
$ bench new-site testsite
$ bench --site testsite install-app maia

Login to your site to configure the app.
